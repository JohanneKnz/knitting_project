What is a knit along?

In a knit along, we get together and knit the same projects at the same time. The Knots and Loops knit alongs are organized with several live sessions on social media, where all the participants come together and share the joy of creating as well as offer guiadance to eachother during the projects.


The knit alongs are online, and usually follow recepies that are availiable in several languages and can be dowloaded free of cost when you have signed up for the Knots and Loops knit alongs before the knit along starts.


Every thrid month, Knots and Loops lead a knit along together with passionate cratives accross the world. The knit alongs are free and inclusive. All you need is yarn, needles and a love for all things creative.

Getting started

Knots and Loops will annouce the project and what materials are required two weeks in advance. Updates during the knit along are posted on our social media accounts.


Signing up

To sign up for your first knit along, fill in your prefernces in the form below. You will get an email confirming your registration, and we will let you know when the next knit along matching your project prefernces is planned.


Support

If you need support with your knit along project or have general questions about knitting, check out out our tutorials page, where you can also request to be matched with a mentor (subject to availiability).
